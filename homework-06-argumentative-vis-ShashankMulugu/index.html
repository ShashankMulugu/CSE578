This visualization consists of two sides which shows the approval and disapproval ratings of President Donald Trump. The source of the data is fivethirtyeight. Here, I have developed two charts one using traditional methods without much manipulation to push a narrative and the other one is desgined to be misleading and pushes a narrative that the President has been gaining approval over the years. The trick used in the chart on the left is usually very commonly used in the news media and appears regularly on r/dataisugly. To make the bar chart, I began by cherrypicking the data. Instead of using the 16,000 surveys in the dataset, I have actually only used 8 which were the top highest approval ratings that the President received. So, in this graph it is implied that the approval ratings are averaged out to the year. But it is not mentioned anywhere in the chart itself. So, this could be one way to show the trends that are not there in the data.
